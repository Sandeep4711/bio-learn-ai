Frequently Asked Questions

To start your first learning path:

  1. Go to the Learning Path section from the sidebar
  2. Choose from available topics or create a custom path
  3. Set your learning goals and preferences
  4. Click "Start Learning" to begin your journey

Quiz scores are calculated based on:

  • Correct answers (weighted by difficulty)
  • Time taken to complete the quiz
  • Number of attempts
  • Bonus points for streaks and improvements

Your progress is tracked through:

  • Dashboard overview with key metrics

If you're having difficulty:

  • Check the additional resources provided

To update your profile:

  1. Click on your Profile in the sidebar
  2. Click the "Edit" button at the top
  3. Update your personal information
  4. Save your changes by clicking "Save Changes"

Your learning streak represents:

  • Breaks if you miss a day of activity
  • Earn bonus points for maintaining streaks
  • Track your consistency and commitment to learning
